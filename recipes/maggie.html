Maggie Recipe

Description

Masala Maggi is a zesty version of our classic noodles. It is made by adding a hint of spices and an array of crunchy vegetables which makes it a sumptuous meal. You can serve this tongue-tickling recipe on game nights and movie marathon days.

Ingredients

  • Maggie
  • Maggie masala
  • Red chilli powder
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil 400ml water
  2. Put maggie in it. Let it cook for a minute
  3. Add all masala and salt
  4. Stir continuously for another minute
  5. Serve hot
